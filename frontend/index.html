Replate
Smarter surplus food matching.

Replate helps restaurants, events, and kitchens redistribute surplus food by matching them with the most suitable NGOs using real constraints, live response logic, and AI reasoning.

Step 1 — Create a donation request

Enter the source, food type, quantity, expiry window, and pickup location from the donor side.

This gives the system the real-world context it needs.

Step 2 — Replate evaluates the options

The backend checks nearby NGOs, compares capacity, distance, reliability, and food fit, then uses AI to explain the best choice.

You get one recommended NGO plus alternatives.

Step 3 — Review and act

The best NGO is shown first, alternatives are listed below, and the pickup plan is generated so the donation can move fast.

This is the actual decision output of the system.
